## PART II — WHY INTERNAL COHERENCE
## ALONE PROVES NOTHING

```

coherent.

coherent. The epicycles are mathematically
consistent with each other. The framework
makes predictions. The predictions are
derivable from the premises.
It is also wrong.

Phlogiston theory is internally coherent.
The mechanism is consistently applied
across cases. The predictions follow from
the premises.
It is also wrong.

Internal coherence is a necessary condition
for a framework to be worth considering.
It is not sufficient to establish that the
framework is correct.

This point matters because it means the
following argument is NOT being made here:

  "The framework is internally consistent,
  therefore it is correct."

The argument being made is different.
And stronger.
```
