## What This Document Is

This document covers every strategy that
does not require you to be in a room,
on a phone, or sending a targeted email.

Passive strategies are not inferior to
active ones. They are different in kind.

Active strategies (in-person visits,
targeted emails) produce direct responses
from specific people. High-return but
finite — there are only so many Tier 1
targets.

Passive strategies build a surface that
works while you sleep. They make you
findable. They make your work citable.
They attract people you did not know
to look for. They compound over time
in a way that active outreach does not.

You need both.
This document covers the passive half.
