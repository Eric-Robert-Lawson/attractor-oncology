LINEAGE CONTEXT:
  Hepatocytes are the most
  metabolically specialised cells
  in the body.
  They are terminally differentiated.
  They do not divide in normal adult
  liver except in regeneration.
  HCC is an arrest in the hepatocyte
  differentiation programme.
