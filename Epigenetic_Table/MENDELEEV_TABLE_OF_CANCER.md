   Every cancer has a cell of origin.

   The number of hub classes is

   + unknown).
   Therefore the table is finite.
   The derivation of drug targets
   for every cancer in every species
   is a finite computation.
   Not an infinite research programme.
   A finite computation with a known
   algorithm.

   Every empty space in the table
   is a prediction about what must
   exist — either a cancer that
   has not been derived yet, or a
   prevention mechanism that has
   not been developed yet.
   The table does not wait for
   empirical discovery.
   It tells us where to look.

   STRUCTURE INVISIBLE IN HUMAN
   CANCER ALONE.
   The avian entries added three
   new geometry classes (Class III,

   human cancer derivations alone.
   The prevention tier dimension

   examined. The table requires
   cross-species input to be
