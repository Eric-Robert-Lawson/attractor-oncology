## INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE

```
Complete every section.
Do not skip fields.
A failure with complete records
is more valuable to the framework

THE BEFORE-DOCUMENT (Section 1)
must be completed and dated BEFORE
the first dose of tazemetostat.
Vinorelbine or supportive care
may already be running — the
before-document is sealed when
tazemetostat is about to be added.

THE PRIMARY RESPONSE SIGNAL FOR
THIS PROTOCOL IS LEG FUNCTION.

This is the clearest, most direct,
most owner-accessible outcome
signal in the entire bounty system.

The mechanism of tazemetostat in
this cancer is DIFFERENTIATION-
FORCING: it removes the EZH2/
H3K27me3 lock on the MET programme
genes, forcing surviving blastemal
cells to undergo the mesenchymal-
to-epithelial transition they have
been unable to complete. As the
blastemal mass reduces — both from
the cytoreductive agent killing
cells and from tazemetostat forcing
the surviving cells to differentiate
and exit the cell cycle — the
pressure on the sciatic nerve
decreases. When the nerve is
decompressed, leg function returns.

LEG FUNCTION IS THE NERVE
DECOMPRESSION SIGNAL.
It is the direct anatomical read-
out of tumour reduction.

If the bird is using its leg again:
  — The tumour is smaller.
  — The sciatic nerve is
    decompressed.
  — The differentiation push is
    working.

The owner does not need a
radiograph to know this. They
look at the cage and the bird is
standing on two feet.

IMPORTANT: NOT ALL LEG PARALYSIS
IN BUDGERIGARS IS NEPHROBLASTOMA.
Differential diagnoses include:
  — Pododermatitis (bumblefoot) —
    inflammatory, affects the foot
    itself, not the leg
  — Gout (articular) — urate
    crystal deposition in the joint
  — Trauma — fracture
  — Metabolic bone disease
  — Spinal lesion
  — Other renal disease (cyst,
    abscess)

The IHC confirmation (Section 2)
is the definitive diagnostic step.
This protocol applies ONLY to
confirmed nephroblastoma with a
dominant blastemal component.

COMPLETE THE BEFORE-DOCUMENT
BEFORE STARTING TAZEMETOSTAT.
RECORD THE LAMENESS GRADE.
THE IMPROVEMENT IN LAMENESS GRADE
IS THE BOUNTY SIGNAL.
```
