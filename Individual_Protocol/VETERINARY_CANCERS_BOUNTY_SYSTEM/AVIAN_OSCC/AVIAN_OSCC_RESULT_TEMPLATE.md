    — clear improvement
2 = Slightly smaller than baseline
    — some improvement
3 = Same as baseline — no change

    — possible progression

    — clear progression

scale (check perches, cage bars,
food dishes, and around the beak):
1 = No blood visible anywhere
2 = Less blood than before
3 = Same as before

SCORE EATING using this scale:
1 = Eating and cracking seeds
    normally — improved
2 = Slight difficulty but better
    than before
3 = Same difficulty as before
4 = More difficulty than before
5 = Not eating — emergency

1 = Normal daily chattering —
    improved or normal
2 = Quieter than usual
