  — Stops proliferating.
  — Forms the cornified cell
    envelope (a structural
    protein scaffold).
  — Undergoes programmed cell
    death as a non-viable
    structural element.
This is what normal squamous
cells do. The cancer cell is
being forced to do what it was
always supposed to do.

is therefore qualitative as well
as quantitative:
  QUANTITATIVE: the mass is smaller

  QUALITATIVE: the surface of the

working at the cellular level —
even before the size changes.

normally? Write this down.
