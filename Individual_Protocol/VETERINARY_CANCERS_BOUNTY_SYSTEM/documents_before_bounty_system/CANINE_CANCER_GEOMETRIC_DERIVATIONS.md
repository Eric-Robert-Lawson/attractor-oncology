### Step 1 — Cell of Origin

```
Histiocytic sarcoma arises from
tissue histiocytes — specifically
the dendritic cell lineage.

The veterinary pathology literature
is unambiguous:

  IRF8 (Interferon Regulatory
  Factor 8) is expressed in canine
  HS and serves as a diagnostic
  marker of dendritic cell origin.

  RUNX3 cooperates with IRF8
  to specify cDC1 identity.

  PU.1/SPI1 is expressed as a
  broad myeloid/dendritic cell
  identity marker.

  These three markers distinguish
  canine HS from lymphoma and
  other round cell tumours on IHC.
  They are the standard diagnostic
  panel.

Cell of origin:
  Conventional dendritic cell
  type 1 (cDC1) / tissue histiocyte
  progenitor.

  Specifically: the cell that should
  become a mature antigen-presenting
  dendritic cell capable of
  activating CD8+ T cells and
  NK cells in immune surveillance.
```

