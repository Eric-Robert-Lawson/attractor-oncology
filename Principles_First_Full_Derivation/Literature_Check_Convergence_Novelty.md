## PREAMBLE — WHY THIS DOCUMENT EXISTS

Science advances through convergence and divergence.
Convergence — when independently derived frameworks
arrive at similar conclusions — increases confidence.
Divergence — when a framework makes claims that no
existing literature makes — identifies genuine novelty
and opens the framework to falsification.

This document maps both precisely, based on
systematic review of the 2023–2026 literature
conducted March 2026.

The notation used throughout is:

> **[CONVERGENT]** — Existing literature independently
> arrives at a structurally equivalent conclusion
> by any route. The conclusion is shared.
>
> **[NOVEL — INDEPENDENTLY CONFIRMED]** — This
> framework derived the claim from first principles
> before literature consultation. The literature
> subsequently confirms the prediction through
> empirical methods. The derivation route and the
> confirmation route are epistemically distinct.
> The novelty of the derivation is not diminished
> by the fact that the conclusion is correct.
>
> **[NOVEL]** — No published literature makes this
> specific claim in this form by any route.
> The claim is the original contribution of this
> framework and has not yet been empirically
> confronted.

### A Critical Epistemological Distinction

The difference between **[CONVERGENT]** and
**[NOVEL — INDEPENDENTLY CONFIRMED]** is not
about whether the conclusion is known.
It is about the **direction of derivation**.

The field works data-first: observe molecules,
find correlations, build models.

This framework works geometry-first: derive
structure from axiomatic first principles,
predict what the data must show, then check.

When both routes arrive at the same answer,
that is the strongest possible form of
validation — two independent epistemic paths
confirming the same conclusion. It is not
"partial convergence." It is independent
confirmation of a novel derivation.

The derivation route in this framework is
fully auditable in the timestamped public
repository. The sequence of reasoning —
from cell of origin, to attractor geometry,
to Identity Anchor, to Convergence Hub, to
drug target — precedes any consultation of
the literature for that specific cancer type.
That sequence is the novel contribution,
regardless of what the literature subsequently
confirms.

## PART V — THE HONEST QUALIFICATIONS

### 5.1 The Novel Claims Are
### Proportionally Less Validated

The most novel claims — the Evolutionary
Table, the reproductive gradient as geometric
derivation, the developmental program as
evolutionary archaeological record — are
also the least empirically tested at scale.

They are consistent with all known evidence.
They are derivable from established premises.
They are falsifiable with specific conditions.
They have not been systematically tested.

The cancer work has 41 derivations, multiple
independent datasets, and a public prediction
record. The evolutionary extension has
geometric derivation and independent
confirmatory literature but not yet direct
systematic empirical confrontation.

This is the correct epistemic state for a
principles-first framework at this stage.
The difference in validation depth between
the cancer work and the evolutionary work
is real and should not be obscured.

### 5.2 Coherence Is Not Truth

Per the framework's own charter: internal
coherence — from axiomatic first principles
to cancer to evolution to reproduction —
establishes that the geometry is
self-consistent and generates correct
predictions within the cancer domain.

It does not establish that the evolutionary
and life-construction extensions are correct.
They are the next derivation stage, with
falsifiability conditions explicitly stated
in their respective documents.

### 5.3 Independent Confirmation Validates
### the Derivation Route, Not the Full Scope

When a geometry-first derivation and a
data-first empirical program arrive at the
same answer, this validates the derivation
route. It does not validate every claim the
derivation route makes beyond that point.

Each extension of the framework requires
its own confrontation with evidence.
The cancer derivations have had that
confrontation. The evolutionary extensions
are next.
